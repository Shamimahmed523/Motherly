Montly food Chart.

Month: 1

■Food Group- Fruits
Foods to include- Oranges, Apples, Berries Nutritional Value- High in vitamins C and fiber Effects on the Baby- Supports immune function and healthy digestion

■Food Group- Vegetables
Foods to include- Leafy Greens (Spinach, Kale), Carrots Nutritional Value- Rich in vitamins A, C, and folate Effects on the Baby- Important for fetal growth and preventing neural tube defects

■Food Group- Whole Grains
Foods to include- Brown Rice, Oats, Whole Wheat Bread Nutritional Value- Provides fiber and B vitamins Effects on the baby- Helps maintain energy levels and digestive health

■Food Group- Protein
Foods to include- Lean Meat, Fish, Eggs, Beans Nutritional Value- Source of protein and iron Effects on the baby- Supports tissue growth and oxygen transport to the baby

■Food Group- Dairy
Foods to include- Milk, Yogurt, Cheese Nutritional Value- High in calcium and protein Effects on the baby- Essential for the baby's bone and teeth development

■Food Group- Healthy Fats
Foods to include- Nuts, Seeds, Avocado Nutritional Value- Provides essential fatty acids Effects on baby- Important for brain development and overall growth
