Montly food Chart.

Month: 2

■Food Group- Fruits
Foods to include- Bananas, Berries, Apples Nutritional Value- High in vitamins, potassium, and fiber Effects on the baby- Supports brain development and digestion

■Food Group- Vegetables
Foods to include- Broccoli, Sweet Potatoes, Bell Peppers Nutritional Value- Rich in vitamins A, C, and fiber Effects on the baby- Important for immune function and skin development

■Food Group- Whole Grains
Foods to include- Quinoa, Barley, Whole Wheat Pasta Nutritional Value- Provides fiber and B vitamins Effects on the baby- Helps maintain energy levels and digestive health

■Food Group- Protein
Foods to include- Chicken, Fish, Lentils, Nuts Nutritional Value- Source of protein, omega-3 fatty acids, and iron Effects on the baby- Supports muscle development and brain growth

■Food Group- Dairy
Foods to include- Yogurt, Milk, Cheese Nutritional Value- High in calcium and protein Effects on the baby- Essential for the baby's bone and teeth development

■Food Group- Healthy Fats
Foods to include- Olive Oil, Avocado, Nut Butters Nutritional Value- Provides essential fatty acids Effects on the baby- Important for brain development and overall growth
